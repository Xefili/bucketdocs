Getting Started

To get started with Bucket Helper, request you personalized version from UphillBucket687#7175 and add the bot to your server. In the following pages, we are going to dive deeper into what Bucket Helper is capable of. All commands prefixed by a * cannot be executed with the prefix.(- Usage*: x)

Getting Help

Bucket Helper has a help command, executed by %help. The prefix may vary, depending on the requested configuration of the bot.(Default: %)
